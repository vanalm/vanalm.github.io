About Me

I am a Maui-based software engineer and entrepreneur with a passion for AI/ML, specializing in 0-1 products. The art and science of bringing ideas into reality particularly inspires me. I love the process of articulating the apperature of focus on a project. For example, starting with clarification of a high-level objective and drawing that mission down into all levels of the project design.

Favorite Books So Far This Year:

  • This Is Marketing: You Can't Be Seen Until You Learn to See- Seth Godin This book was inspiring and I recommend it to everyone. To me it was a manifesto for human connection and for empathy driven innovation.

  • Getting Past No: Negotiating in Difficult Situations- William Ury. If I'm honest, I started this book because I heard it referenced by Tim Ferriss and I liked the idea of being a smarter negotiator. I got more value than expected: another reminder that leading with empathy is the most effective route forward in any situation. This book was packed with maybe 75:25 practical tools to philosophy.

  • Complexity: The Emerging Science at the Edge of Order and Chaos, M. Mitchell Waldrop - This was absolutely inspiring to read, and I had to constantly remind myself that it was published in 1992! I finished this book feeling hungry for discovery and creating, and inspired by the roots of the AI field that I love so much. It also inspired a new curiousity in economics...

  • Build: An Unorthodox Guide to Making Things Worth Making, Tony Fadell This book is full of invaluable insights into the journey of entrepreneurship.

  • The Lean Startup by Eric Ries - This was eye opening for me. What we are driven to build and what we want are not always lined up. This book is a guide to aligning daily activities to end goals

  • Drive: The Surprising Truth About What Motivates Us, Daniel H. Pink. I listened to this one, and gained some reinforcing insights about what motivates and demovitates me and the people I work with.

CocoWireless

CocoWireless is a surf and weather report tool that users can access through the native messaging app on any phone. It is an LLM based app that provides realtime information through notifications or chat. Version 1 was built on Heroku, V2 in the AWS ecosystem with a serverless architecture. I manage state and conversation history manually, and perform simple and effective RAG operations. Check it out at www.cocowireless.org.
