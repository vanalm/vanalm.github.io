About Me

I am a Maui-based engineer and entrepreneur with a background in data science and machine learning and a B.S. in Plant Science from Cornell University. I have experience in the fields of medicine, agriculture, construction, and technology. I am passionate about creating solutions that are both practical and sustainable and am currently working on a number of projects in this vein, including Maui Farm and Home LLC, CocoWireless LLC, and various machine learning projects. I am always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow, and I am excited to see where my journey will take me next.

Maui Farm and Home

Maui Farm and Home LLC is a business focused on enhancing the aesthetics and efficiency of properties and systems. Leveraging a unique combination of high-tech and trades expertise, we are uniquely positioned to assist both small farm businesses and property owners with building, maintaining, and improving their properties and systems. Our handyman services include construction, renovation, repair, design, off-grid internet and electrical solutions, and more. Our tech solutions include modeling and simulation, Iot sensors, web development/dashboards, irrigation design and assessment, crop diagnosis, data collection and management strategies, and more.

CocoWireless

CocoWireless is a surf and weather report tool that users can access through the native messaging app on any phone. It is an LLM based app that provides realtime information through notifications or chat. Version 1 was built on Heroku, V2 in the AWS ecosystem with a serverless architecture. I manage state and conversation history manually, and perform simple and effective RAG operations. Check it out at www.cocowireless.org.
