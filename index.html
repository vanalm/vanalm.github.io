About Me

I am a Maui-based engineer and entrepreneur with a background in data science and machine learning and a B.S. in Plant Science from Cornell University. I have experience in the fields of medicine, agriculture, construction, and technology. I am passionate about creating solutions that are both practical and sustainable and am currently working on a number of projects in this vein, including Maui Farm and Home LLC, CocoWireless LLC, and various machine learning projects. I am always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow, and I am excited to see where my journey will take me next.

Maui Farm and Home LLC

Maui Farm and Home LLC is a registered and insured handyman business that I started post-COVID, where I focus on turning my community's 'I should get that done someday' projects into sources of satisfaction and joy. I tackle a broad spectrum of tasks from small construction and remodel projects, to the integration of off-grid solutions, including solar electric systems and high-speed Ubiquiti internet systems. Drawing on my strong roots in agriculture, I also provide agricultural consulting services. Much like Marie Kondo, who advocates for environments that spark joy, through Maui Farm and Home LLC, I aim to transform properties into a spaces that do just that.

CocoWireless

CocoWireless is a text message information service. It is an LM based technology that provides realtime information about local Maui weather and wave data, reports and forecasts as well as alerts, county press release summaries, local event info, and more. Version 1 was built on Heroku, V2 in the AWS ecosystem with a serverless architeture. I manage state and conversation history manually, and perform simple and effective RAG operations. Check it out at www.cocowireless.org.
