About Me

As a passionate innovator and lifelong learner, I've charted a unique path through various realms of science and engineering, consistently weaving a thread of innovation through each endeavor. My journey began at Cornell University, where I delved into the intricacies of plant sciences, coupled with rigorous coursework in physics, engineering, and biology. This diverse academic foundation laid the groundwork for my multifaceted career, fostering a mindset geared towards creative and innovative solutions.

Starting in the realm of healthcare, I created and led a delirium abatement project team, and carried out hospital wide experiments to assesss disease control practices, applying my gumption and desire to understand and improve. This initiative marked the beginning of my journey in transforming theoretical knowledge into innovative practices. Similarly, in agricultural technology, I managed a pioneering commercial hydroponic berry growing project. Here, I combined my love of biology with cutting-edge agricultural technology, innovatively addressing physiological challenges in raspberries and using practical data from the field to steer production tasks, thereby sewing a passion for data science.

My curiosity quickly grew into the world of neuroscience, as evidenced by my meticulous study of Kandel's Principles of Neural Sciences and my visiting and interviewing several neuroscientists throughout the US and Germany. At McLean Hospital's Low Field Magnetic Stimulation group, I contributed not just to organizingn data and circuit board designs, but also to strategizing development paths and clinical designs.

The 2020 shutdown marked a pivotal moment for me, sparking the creation of my own handyman business. This venture was not just a display of entrepreneurial spirit but also a testament to my ability to innovate under changing circumstances. It allowed me the flexibility to dive deeper into my passion for technology, where I immersed myself in machine learning. Completing MITx courses in probabilistic system analysis and python development, I engaged in a plethora of machine learning projects ranging from deep learning to traditional methods, applying deep learning techniques to predict wave heights, among other practical innovations.

At the heart of my journey lies a deep-seated passion for creation and problem-solving. Whether it's modeling systems, developing full-stack solutions in Python, or navigating the exciting world of machine learning, I thrive on exploring new challenges and pushing the boundaries of what's possible. My diverse experiences, from introducing practical innovations for small farmers on the island of Maui to advancing technology in various fields, have shaped me into a versatile leader, innovator, and engineer, grounded in the power of deep, strategic thinking across various domains.

Maui Farm and Home LLC

Maui Farm and Home LLC is a registered and insured handyman business that I started post-COVID, where I focus on turning my community's 'I should get that done someday' projects into sources of satisfaction and joy. I tackle a broad spectrum of tasks from small construction and remodel projects, to the integration of off-grid solutions, including solar electric systems and high-speed Ubiquiti internet systems. Drawing on my strong roots in agriculture, I also provide agricultural consulting services. Much like Marie Kondo, who advocates for environments that spark joy, through Maui Farm and Home LLC, I aim to transform properties into a spaces that do just that.

CocoWireless

CocoWireless is a text message information service. It is an LM based service that provides realtime information about local Maui weather and wave data, reports and forecasts as well as alerts, county press release summaries, local event info, and more. Version 1 was built on Heroku, V2 in the AWS ecosystem with a serverless architeture. Check out the [very basic] website at www.cocowireless.org.
